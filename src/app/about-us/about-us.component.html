About Me
I live in Hong Kong and have closely witnessed the sudden dawn of COVID-19 disease (earlier known as novel Corona Virus) in this beautiful city. I also stand testimony to how Hongkongers have managed to keep infection rates low by practicing good hygiene and keeping themselves informed as this situation progresses.

The first case of COVID-19 in India was confirmed on 30 January 2020. Given the disease becoming increasingly threatening, I decided to develop this portal so that people can get the latest information on the situation in India.

Other than sharing the trait of indecipherable hand-writing with doctors, I am no medical expert myself. Information you see on this website is sourced strictly from Government and other verified agencies.

I released the initial version of this website with a weekend's worth of work. I have a regular day-job and manage to add new features to this website in the evenings (including this lengthy essay). Thankfully, I wouldn't have to sacrifice a lot many evenings as I am hopeful the situation will get better in the coming weeks as people become more aware and exercise precautions. Also, I am very optimistic about the medical community developing a vaccine or drug or both very soon.

This website is powered by open source technologies (see below) and numerous cups of Indian and Turkish tea. I do not intend to put any advertisements on this portal. Nor do I seek donations.
